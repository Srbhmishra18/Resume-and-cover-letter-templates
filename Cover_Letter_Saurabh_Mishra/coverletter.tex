\begin{letter}
I am writing to apply for the Junior Data Analyst / Business Intelligence Analyst (m/w/d) position in Meerbusch.
\lettersection{About Me}
As a Data Analyst with over four years of experience in manufacturing at Husqvarna and Bosch, I specialize in SQL‑based analysis, ETL workflows, KPI design, and automated reporting using Power BI. Holding a Master’s in Data Science from Trier University (completed 2025), with a focus on machine learning, statistics, and database management, I am certified as a Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Associate (PL‑300). I bring a structured and analytical mindset, good SQL skills, and hands‑on experience in BI reporting and process automation.

\lettersection{Why ISEKI-Maschinen GmbH and This Particular Job?}
I am particularly drawn to ISEKI-Maschinen GmbH as a leading manufacturer in the agricultural machinery sector. Having managed supply chain data and reporting at Gardena GmbH (Husqvarna Group), another production-focused machinery company, I understand how critical accurate KPIs, data quality, and automated dashboards are for supporting business and finance decisions in this industry. The Junior Data Analyst / BI Analyst role, with its focus on data governance, standardization, and reporting, feels like a natural next step.

\lettersection{Why Me?}
My background aligns closely with your needs: At Gardena, I redesigned the reporting ecosystem from Excel-based processes to structured Power BI solutions with proper data modeling and ETL pipelines. This reduced data processing time by 40\% and provided senior stakeholders with faster, more reliable insights. In my Advanced SQL Business Analytics project, I built PostgreSQL data layers with cohort and Pareto analyses - directly transferable to user-behaviour tracking and deep-dive analyses.

I am fully available for full-time work in Meerbusch and would be excited to bring this practical experience to ISEKI’s team.

\end{letter}
